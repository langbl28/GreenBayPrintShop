Sustainability

We use water-based sustainable ink and encourage shirts that are US made and sweatshop free.

Small, Independent & Local

We are a local print shop located on the east side of Green Bay with a small team but a large passion for screen printing.

Quality Productions

We pride ourselves on making quality prints that are made to last. All of our garments are hand printed. We encourage use of sustainable and sweatshop free garments. It's important to us that we create shirts you actually want to wear.

Flexibility is Key

Whether you are looking for 5 shirts or 5,000 we will work with you from design to delivery to make sure you are getting exactly what you are looking for. Our goal is to make this as smooth of a process as possible and we offer in house design work which helps allow us to be flexible with a quick turn around. It means you to spend less time worrying and more time where you want to be with great apparel.

Competitive Pricing

There are no hidden fees, no surprises and no minimums - our pricing model does away with screen, art, ink change and extra fees making this a straightforward process to get you exactly what you need. Our pricing varies on quantity and style of garment. Every order is unique and we work hard to create a personalized catalogue for each customer we work with. Have questions or need a quote? Please contact us and we will be happy to walk you through the process!
